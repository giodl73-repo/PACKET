# Anchor Institution

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures schools, libraries, and health facilities that
both consume high-capacity connectivity and serve community access.

## Primary concerns

- High-capacity, reliable service for many users (DIM-02/05).
- Middle-mile and interconnection to support demand (DIM-08/10).
- Role as a community access point bridging the divide (DIM-11).

## Used during

- Capacity, middle-mile, and equity scoring; tier/SLA for anchor/aggregation (T3).
