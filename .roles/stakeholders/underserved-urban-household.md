# Underserved Urban Household

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures urban households nominally "served" but priced out
or stuck on inadequate service.

## Primary concerns

- Affordability and adoption, not just coverage (DIM-06/11).
- Speed adequate for school, work, and telehealth (DIM-02/03).
- Competition to discipline price (DIM-07).

## Used during

- Affordability, equity, and competition scoring; tier/SLA for last-mile.
