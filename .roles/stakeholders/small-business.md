# Small Business

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures small businesses dependent on reliable
connectivity for operations, payments, and cloud services.

## Primary concerns

- Uptime and reliability under load (DIM-05/03).
- Symmetric capacity for cloud/upload (DIM-02).
- A second path or provider for continuity (DIM-04/07).

## Used during

- Reliability, capacity, and redundancy scoring; tier/SLA for business access.
