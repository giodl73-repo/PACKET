# Content / Cloud Provider

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures content and cloud providers that interconnect with
access networks to deliver services to users.

## Primary concerns

- Peering and interconnection capacity and quality (DIM-10).
- Latency to end users (DIM-03).
- Diverse, congestion-free paths into the access network (DIM-04).

## Used during

- Interconnection, latency, and topology scoring; tier/SLA for backbone/exchange
  (T1/T2).
