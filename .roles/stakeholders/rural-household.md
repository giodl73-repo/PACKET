# Rural Household

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures households in unserved or underserved rural areas.

## Primary concerns

- Any adequate fixed service at all (DIM-01), not just a map claim.
- Affordability and reliability of what is available (DIM-06/05).
- Whether middle-mile exists to make last-mile possible (DIM-08).

## Used during

- Coverage, affordability, and middle-mile scoring; tier/SLA for last-mile (T4).
